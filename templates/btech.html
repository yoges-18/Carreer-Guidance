B.Tech - Engineering Branches

Explore different branches available under the B.Tech (Science) stream.

Computer Science Engineering (CSE)

Focuses on programming, software development, AI, cybersecurity, and data science.

Mechanical Engineering

Deals with design and manufacturing of machines, automotive, and thermal systems.

Electrical Engineering

Covers circuits, power systems, electronics, control systems, and electromagnetics.

Civil Engineering

Focuses on infrastructure like buildings, bridges, roads, and construction management.

Electronics & Communication

Combines electronics with communication technologies like networks, signals, and IoT.

Information Technology

Focuses on information systems, software, web technologies, and data management.
