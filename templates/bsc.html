Commerce Stream

Discover top courses and careers in the Commerce stream.

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

Foundational course in accounting, economics, business law, and taxation. Leads to CA, MBA, or banking careers.

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

Focuses on business management, HR, marketing, and entrepreneurship. Ideal for future managers and leaders.

Chartered Accountancy (CA)

Professional certification in accounting and auditing. Highly respected and offers great career opportunities.

Company Secretary (CS)

Specializes in corporate law, company governance, and regulatory compliance. Often pursued with B.Com.

Bachelor of Economics (B.Econ)

Focuses on economic theory, finance, and policy-making. Ideal for research, planning, and government roles.
